Solution:

 

     Some solutions for depression is to be happy. You can achieve this if you talk with someone even at least your family, don’t hide your feelings and what you feel. You should talk to someone about it if your feeling stress or depressed. You can also go outside and take a short walk around your neighborhood, exercising is another way. Taking anti depressant drugs can also help reduce your depression you might take that in consideration if your feeling depress. Challenging your negative thoughts a lot of the work in treating depression is changing how you think. When you're depressed, you embrace worst-case scenarios and obsessively dwell on the negative.
